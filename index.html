What is ClubBath?

ClubBath is UoB's 100% unofficial guide to nightlife in the our great city.

Whether you love a MNB or are more about The Big Cheese, you can rate your experience and share it with other club fans!

OUR RATING SYSTEM

When submitting an entry, 6 key elements are taken into account to give an overall score out of 10:

  • Music
  • Size
  • Price
  • Location
  • Toilets
  • and most important of all,

    • A SPECIAL MENTION

something that really makes the night out (somewhat) memorable.

So feel free to leave a submission below and let us know about your favourite club experiences.
